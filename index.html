About Me

I got hooked on HTML code during my 11-year role as a communications specialist with American Express. I designed and built several user-friendly websites that employees raved about. To keep the pages enticing, I added fresh content daily and did user experience testing once a year to create the best user experience possible. Each of the websites I managed landed in the top five percent of the most popular American Express sites.

Recently, I returned to school to expand on my web designer and development skills to learn HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, GitHub, and responsive web design. I added in a splash of Adobe Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator knowledge as well as React and CSS Grid. That's what I love about the tech world - there are always new skills to learn and add to my digital toolbox!
